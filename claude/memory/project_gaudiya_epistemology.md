Aditya wrote (as of 2026-03-28) a philosophical essay exploring the epistemological tension in Gaudiya Vaishnavism: scriptural authority (shabda) is held as supreme proof, but the justification for shabda's supremacy itself relies on inference (anumana), making the argument potentially self-undermining. Also addresses conflicts with empirical science (pratyaksha).

**Why:** Deep personal engagement with reconciling his Vedic tradition's epistemology with his empirical/scientific worldview. Not rejecting tradition — seeking honest intellectual integration.
**How to apply:** This is a live intellectual thread. When discussing philosophy, epistemology, or Vedic studies, he values rigorous analysis over devotional platitudes. He holds both scientific skepticism and openness to the transcendent.
