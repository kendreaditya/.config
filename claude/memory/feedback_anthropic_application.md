When drafting "Why Anthropic?" or similar application essays, write authentically and values-driven, NOT polished metrics-heavy pitches.

**Why:** Aditya's casual, philosophical answer about wanting meaningful work, AI as the most powerful lever, and shifting quality of life distribution got a response. My initial polished answer focused on growth metrics from Meta and was wrong for the tone.

**How to apply:** For Anthropic applications, lead with genuine personal reflection, mention effective altruism, AI safety philosophy, Amanda Askell's work on Claude personality. Keep it casual and honest. Don't over-optimize for keywords. For other companies (Meta, TikTok, etc.), the metrics-heavy approach may still be appropriate — this feedback is Anthropic-specific.
